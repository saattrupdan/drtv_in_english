## Short description (≤132 characters)
English subtitles for DR TV — client-side LLM translation, your own API key.

## Long description
DRTV in English adds English subtitles to DR's video player using client-side translation. When enabled, the extension captures Danish subtitle text from DR's player, sends it to your chosen LLM for translation, and displays English subtitles as an overlay track.

Everything runs locally in your browser — no data is collected or stored by us. Your API key stays on your machine and is only used to contact your chosen LLM provider.

Choose your preferred LLM provider, add your API key in the extension options, and click the subtitle toggle on any DRTV episode to get English subtitles instantly.
